<p><p>Air sanders are power tools used in woodworking and metalworking for sanding and smoothing surfaces. There are two main types of air sanders - orbital sanders and belt sanders. Orbital sanders have a circular sanding pad that moves in a random orbital pattern, making them ideal for finishing tasks. Belt sanders have a continuous loop of sandpaper that moves in a linear fashion, making them suitable for removing large amounts of material quickly. Both types of air sanders are commonly used in workshops and by professional tradespeople.</p></p>
