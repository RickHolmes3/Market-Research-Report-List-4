<p><p>Apheresis machines consist of two main market types: donor apheresis and therapeutic apheresis. Donor apheresis machines are used to collect specific blood components from a donor, such as platelets or plasma, for transfusion to patients in need. Therapeutic apheresis machines, on the other hand, are used to treat various medical conditions by removing harmful substances from the patient's blood, such as toxins or autoantibodies. Both types of machines play crucial roles in healthcare settings to support blood transfusions and therapeutic treatments.</p></p>
