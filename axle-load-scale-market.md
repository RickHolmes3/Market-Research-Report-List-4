<p><p>There are two main types of axle load scales: fixed axle load scales and portable axle load scales. Fixed axle load scales are permanently installed on the ground and are typically used at weigh stations or checkpoints. Portable axle load scales, on the other hand, can be moved and set up at different locations as needed. They are often used for temporary weight measurement purposes, such as monitoring weight limits on construction sites or for emergency vehicle inspections.</p></p>
