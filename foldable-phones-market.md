<p><p>Foldable phones are classified into two main types based on their folding mechanism: In-folding Type and Out-folding Type. In-folding Type foldable phones feature a screen that folds inwards, protecting the display when closed. These phones typically have a larger outer screen for regular use when the device is unfolded. Out-folding Type foldable phones, on the other hand, have a screen that folds outwards, creating a seamless larger display when unfolded. Both types offer unique design and functionality options for consumers looking for innovative mobile devices.</p></p>
