<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li><li>Magneto-Dynamic Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles use various technologies to transfer power without the need for physical cables. Electromagnetic induction works by creating a magnetic field between a transmitter and receiver coil. Magnetic resonance allows for more flexible positioning of the transmitter and receiver coils. Magneto-dynamic coupling uses a rotating magnetic field to transfer power between the charging pad and the vehicle. Each technology offers its own advantages and limitations, catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
