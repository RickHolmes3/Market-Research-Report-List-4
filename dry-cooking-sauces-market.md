<p><p>Dry cooking sauces are convenient options for consumers looking to add flavor to their dishes without the hassle of preparing sauces from scratch. There are three main types of dry cooking sauces: dehydrated sauce, gravy mixture, and pre-made gravy granules. Dehydrated sauces are in powder form and require mixing with water or other liquids to create a sauce. Gravy mixtures are pre-portioned combinations of spices and thickeners that can be added to dishes. Pre-made gravy granules are ready-to-use powders that can be sprinkled directly onto food.</p></p>
