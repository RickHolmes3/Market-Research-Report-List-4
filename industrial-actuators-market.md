<p><p>Industrial actuators can be categorized into two main types: automatic electric actuators and drive electric actuators. Automatic electric actuators are designed to automatically control valves, dampers, and other industrial processes without human intervention. Drive electric actuators, on the other hand, are used to manually control industrial processes by converting electrical energy into mechanical motion. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in various industries by providing precise and efficient control over a wide range of mechanical systems.</p></p>
