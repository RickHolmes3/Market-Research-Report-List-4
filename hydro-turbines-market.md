<p><p>Hydro turbines can be categorized into different types based on their design and application. The Francis turbine is known for its high efficiency at medium head levels, while the Kaplan turbine is suitable for both low and high head installations. The Pelton turbine, on the other hand, is ideal for high head and low flow conditions. Apart from these main types, there are also other variations like propeller and tubular turbines that cater to specific requirements in the hydro power industry.</p></p>
