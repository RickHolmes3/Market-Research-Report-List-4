<p><p>Data Center Transformers are essential components in data center infrastructure which are used to step up or step down the voltage to match the requirements of IT equipment. There are two main types of transformers used in data centers, namely Dry Type and Liquid Type. </p><p>Dry Type transformers are maintenance-free and environmentally-friendly as they do not contain any liquid coolant. Liquid Type transformers, on the other hand, use oil or other liquid coolant for insulation and cooling purposes, which make them more efficient in managing heat dissipation.</p></p>
