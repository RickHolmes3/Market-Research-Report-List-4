<p><p>The floating solar system market consists of various types of components including PV modules, floating bodies and anchoring systems, inverters, and others. PV modules are the solar panels that convert sunlight into electricity. Floating bodies and anchoring systems are used to keep the solar panels afloat on water bodies. Inverters are devices that convert the direct current (DC) generated by the solar panels into alternating current (AC) for use in homes and businesses. Other components in this market include cables, monitoring systems, and control systems.</p></p>
