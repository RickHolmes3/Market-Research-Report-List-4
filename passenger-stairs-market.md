<p><p>Passenger stairs are equipment used to board and disembark passengers from airplanes at airports. There are two main types of passenger stairs on the market: self-propelled and towable. Self-propelled passenger stairs have their own engine for movement, providing flexibility and ease of use. Towable passenger stairs are designed to be towed by a vehicle, offering a more cost-effective option for smaller airports or limited budgets. Both types of passenger stairs serve the same purpose of facilitating passenger boarding and disembarkation from aircraft.</p></p>
