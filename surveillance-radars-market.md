This allows for more accurate tracking of targets in three-dimensional space, making them ideal for military applications.</p><p>4D radars incorporate the element of time, providing a more comprehensive understanding of the target's movement over time. This advanced technology is used for precision tracking in defense and security systems.</p></p>
