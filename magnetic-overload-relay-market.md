<p><p>Magnetic overload relays are commonly available in two main types: electronic and dashpot relays. Electronic overload relays use electronic circuits to monitor the current passing through a motor and can provide more precise and flexible protection settings. On the other hand, dashpot overload relays contain mechanical components such as a dashpot to provide time delay and dampening for better overload protection. Both types of relays are used in various industrial applications to protect motors from overheating and overloading, ensuring smooth and efficient operation.</p></p>
