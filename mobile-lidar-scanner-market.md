<p><strong>The Mobile LiDAR Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Solid-state</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical LiDAR scanners typically use rotating mirrors to capture multiple angles for 3D mapping. These scanners are durable and have long-range capabilities, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Solid-state LiDAR scanners, on the other hand, use solid-state components like microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) to create a 3D map. These scanners are more compact, lightweight, and energy-efficient compared to mechanical scanners, making them ideal for small, portable devices. Both types of scanners cater to different market needs based on factors like range, resolution, and cost.</p></p>
