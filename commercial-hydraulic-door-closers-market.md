<p><p>The commercial hydraulic door closers market can be classified into three main types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor spring door closers. Surface applied door closers are installed on the surface of the door and frame, while concealed door closers are hidden within the door or frame for a more streamlined appearance. Floor spring door closers are mounted in the floor underneath the door, providing a controlled closing mechanism. Each type offers different features and benefits to suit various commercial applications.</p></p>
