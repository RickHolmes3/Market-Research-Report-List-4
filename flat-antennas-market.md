<p><p>Flat antennas refer to antennas with a low profile design suitable for various applications. Electronically-steered antennas are equipped with phase shifters to steer the beam electronically, providing fast and accurate signal tracking. Mechanically-steered antennas, on the other hand, use physical movement to adjust the direction of the beam. Both types of antennas have their own advantages and are used in different industries based on their specific requirements for signal tracking and alignment.</p></p>
