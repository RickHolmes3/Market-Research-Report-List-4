<p><strong>The Machine Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision cameras are classified into two main types: CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras use charge-coupled devices to capture images by converting light into electrical signals. They offer high image quality and are ideal for applications that require precise and accurate imaging. On the other hand, CMOS cameras use complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering lower power consumption and faster processing speeds. They are more cost-effective and suitable for applications that require real-time data processing. Both types of cameras have their advantages and are used for various industrial and commercial purposes.</p></p>
