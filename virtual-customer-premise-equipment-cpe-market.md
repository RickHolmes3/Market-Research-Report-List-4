<p><p>Virtual Customer Premise Equipment (CPE) market has two main types: Classic CPE and Cloud Hosted CPE. Classic CPE involves physical devices installed at the customer's location to manage and deliver services. On the other hand, Cloud Hosted CPE operates virtually with services and applications hosted in the cloud, eliminating the need for physical devices on-premise. Classic CPE is suitable for traditional setups, while Cloud Hosted CPE offers scalability and flexibility for businesses looking to leverage cloud technology.</p></p>
