<p><p>Differential Thermal Analysis (DTA) is a technique used to analyze the temperature difference between a sample and a reference material as they undergo a controlled heating process. Single-function DTA instruments are designed solely for this purpose, while multi-function instruments combine DTA with other techniques such as thermogravimetric analysis or differential scanning calorimetry. Single-function DTA instruments cater to laboratories focused on thermal analysis, while multi-function instruments cater to a broader range of applications due to their versatility and ability to provide more comprehensive analytical data.</p></p>
