<p><p>AC Response Accelerometers are sensors used to measure acceleration in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: Charge Mode Piezoelectric and Voltage Mode Piezoelectric. </p><p>Charge Mode Piezoelectric accelerometers generate an electrical charge in response to acceleration, which is measured and converted into an output signal. Voltage Mode Piezoelectric accelerometers, on the other hand, generate a voltage output directly proportional to the acceleration experienced.</p><p>Both types offer high sensitivity and accuracy, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
