<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water Vapor Cryocooler Market is segmented into two types: Closed Loop and Open Loop systems. Closed Loop systems cycle the working fluid continuously within a closed loop, providing a more efficient cooling process with minimal maintenance requirements. Open Loop systems, on the other hand, release the working fluid into the environment after use, requiring regular refills and posing potential environmental risks. While Closed Loop systems are more popular for their sustainability and efficiency, Open Loop systems are used in certain applications where high cooling capacities are required.</p></p>
