<p><strong>The Offline 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li><li>PMP-based 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offline 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment market consists of two main types - Laser-based and PMP-based systems. Laser-based equipment utilizes laser technology to accurately scan and measure solder paste deposits on PCBs. PMP-based systems, on the other hand, use Projection Moiré Profilometry to analyze solder paste volume and height. Both types of equipment are essential for ensuring high-quality soldering in electronics manufacturing processes by providing precise measurements and detecting defects in solder paste deposits.</p></p>
