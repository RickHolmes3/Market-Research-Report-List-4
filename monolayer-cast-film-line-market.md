<p><p>Monolayer Cast Film Lines, specifically CPP Film Lines and CPE Film Lines, are types of machinery used in the manufacturing process of producing cast films. CPP Film Lines are used to create Cast Polypropylene films, which offer excellent clarity, strength, and heat sealing properties. On the other hand, CPE Film Lines are used for producing Cast Polyethylene films, known for their toughness, flexibility, and resistance to moisture. Both types of film lines play a crucial role in the packaging industry for various applications.</p></p>
