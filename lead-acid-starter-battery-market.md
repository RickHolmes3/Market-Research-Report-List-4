<p><p>The Lead Acid Starter Battery Market includes three main types: General Storage Battery, Dry Charged Battery, and Maintenance Free Battery. General Storage Batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries that require periodic electrolyte maintenance. Dry Charged Batteries come without electrolyte and require filling before use. Maintenance Free Batteries are sealed and do not require refilling or electrolyte maintenance. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences, offering a variety of options for consumers in the market for lead-acid starter batteries.</p></p>
