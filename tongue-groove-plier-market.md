<p><p>Tongue and groove pliers are available in two main types in the market: Straight Jaw and Arc Jaw. Straight Jaw pliers have flat, straight jaws that provide a strong grip on flat surfaces. They are commonly used for turning nuts, bolts, and pipes. Arc Jaw pliers, on the other hand, have curved jaws that offer a better grip on irregularly shaped objects. They are ideal for gripping round objects such as pipes and rods. Both types of pliers are essential tools for various mechanical and plumbing tasks.</p></p>
