<p><p>Flow Chemistry market can be segmented into various types based on reactor designs such as Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR), Plug Flow Reactors (PFR), Micro Reactor Systems (MRT), and others. CSTRs maintain a constant volume and are suitable for homogeneous reactions. PFRs offer high conversion rates and are used for continuous reactions. MRTs are small-scale reactors that provide rapid mixing and efficient heat transfer. Other types may include oscillatory flow reactors, electrochemical flow cells, and photochemical flow reactors.</p></p>
