<p><p>Internal Floating Roofs (IFRs) come in various types, such as Metal Disc IFR and Metal Partition IFR. Metal Disc IFRs are made up of multiple steel discs connected together, providing a sealed cover over the liquid surface. On the other hand, Metal Partition IFRs have vertical internal partitions to separate the liquid compartments. Both types help reduce evaporative emissions, improve safety, and maintain the quality of stored liquids in tanks in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and water treatment.</p></p>
