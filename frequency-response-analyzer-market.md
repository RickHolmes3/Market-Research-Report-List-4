<p><p>Frequency response analyzers are used to measure the output of a system in response to different types of input signals. In the sine wave market, analyzers measure the system's frequency response to a pure sine wave signal. Square wave analyzers focus on the response to a signal that alternates between two levels creating a square shape. Triangle wave analyzers assess the system's response to a signal that linearly increases and decreases in amplitude. Each market segment addresses specific testing needs for different types of systems.</p></p>
