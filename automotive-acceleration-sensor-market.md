<p><p>The automotive acceleration sensor market includes various types such as Piezoresistive, Capacitive, and Servo-type sensors. </p><p>Piezoresistive sensors measure changes in resistance due to acceleration, while Capacitive sensors detect changes in capacitance. </p><p>Servo-type sensors use a feedback control system to measure acceleration. </p><p>Piezoresistive sensors are commonly used in automotive applications due to their sensitivity and reliability, while Capacitive sensors are known for their high accuracy and durability. </p><p>Servo-type sensors provide precise measurements and are often used in high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
