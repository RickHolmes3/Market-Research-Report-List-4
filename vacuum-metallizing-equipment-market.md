<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum metallizing equipment market consists of two main types - suspension type and roller type. Suspension type vacuum metallizing equipment involves suspending the objects to be metallized in the vacuum chamber, allowing for a uniform coating. On the other hand, roller type vacuum metallizing equipment involves passing the objects through rollers in the vacuum chamber, ensuring a continuous and consistent coating. Both types are used in various industries for applications such as automotive parts, electronic devices, and decorative coatings.</p></p>
