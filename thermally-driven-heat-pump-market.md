<p><p>Thermally driven heat pump markets consist of various types such as vertical closed loop, horizontal closed loop, open loop, and other markets. Vertical closed loop systems involve drilling deep wells for vertical loops to extract heat from the ground. Horizontal closed loop systems use coils buried horizontally underground to transfer heat. Open loop systems extract heat from a water source such as a lake or pond. Other markets may include hybrid systems that combine different loop designs for optimal efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages for heating and cooling applications.</p></p>
