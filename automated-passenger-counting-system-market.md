<p><p>Automated Passenger Counting System market includes various types such as Infrared, Stereoscopic Vision, Time-of-Flight, and IP Cameras. Infrared systems use sensors to detect heat emitted by passengers. Stereoscopic vision systems utilize two cameras to generate a 3D image of the passenger. Time-of-Flight systems measure the time it takes for light to reflect off an object. IP cameras capture and analyze video footage to count passengers. Each type offers unique advantages and may be suited for different environments or requirements.</p></p>
