<p><p>RGB CMOS image sensors are commonly used in digital cameras and other imaging devices. There are two main types of CMOS image sensor markets based on the shutter mechanism: Global Shutter and Rolling Shutter. Global Shutter captures the entire image at once, resulting in no distortion of moving objects. Rolling Shutter captures the image line by line, which can cause motion blur or distortion of moving subjects. Both markets cater to different industries and applications, offering unique advantages and features for various imaging needs.</p></p>
