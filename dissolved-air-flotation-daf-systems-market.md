<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Systems are water treatment technology used to remove suspended solids, oils, and grease from water. They work by introducing air bubbles into the water, which attach to the particles and float them to the surface for removal. DAF systems are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, food and beverage, and municipal wastewater treatment.</p><p>The Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
