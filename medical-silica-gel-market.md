<p><ul><li>Raw Silica Gel</li><li>Mixing Silica Gel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Raw silica gel is a type of medical silica gel that is in its pure form without any additives or chemicals. It is used for various medical applications due to its high absorption capacity and non-toxic nature. Mixing silica gel, on the other hand, is a blend of raw silica gel with other materials such as desiccants or indicators. This type of silica gel is used in more specialized medical applications where specific properties such as moisture control or color-changing capabilities are required.</p></p>
