<p><ul><li>Inherent Fire Resistant Fabric</li><li>Treated Fire Resistant Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire resistant fabric market can be categorized into two types: Inherent Fire Resistant Fabric and Treated Fire Resistant Fabric. Inherent Fire Resistant Fabric is made with fibers that have fire resistant properties naturally, such as aramid and modacrylic. On the other hand, Treated Fire Resistant Fabric is regular fabric that has been treated with chemicals to provide fire resistance. Both types of fabrics are used in various industries such as firefighting, military, oil & gas, and industrial sectors for protective clothing and equipment.</p></p>
