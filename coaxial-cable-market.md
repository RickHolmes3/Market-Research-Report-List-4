<p><p>Coaxial cable market types include outer plastic sheath, woven copper shield, inner dielectric insulator, and copper core. The outer plastic sheath provides protection and insulation for the cable, while the woven copper shield acts as a barrier against interference. The inner dielectric insulator maintains the signal's integrity by preventing electrical interference. The copper core serves as the conductor for transmitting data signals. Together, these components make up a reliable and high-quality coaxial cable for various applications.</p></p>
