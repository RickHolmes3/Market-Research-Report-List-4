<p><p>Industrial vacuum units are classified into different types based on their working mechanisms. Rotary vane units are widely used and provide high vacuum levels. Diaphragm units are oil-free and suitable for sensitive applications. Liquid ring units are known for their versatility and ability to handle liquid and solid particles. Venturi units rely on compressed air for operation. Piston units are durable and reliable for industrial settings. Turbomolecular units provide extremely high vacuum levels and are commonly used in research and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
