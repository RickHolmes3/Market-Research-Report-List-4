<p><p>District Heating Pipe System market is classified into two main types: Hot Water Heating System and Steam Heating System. Hot Water Heating System utilizes hot water to heat buildings, distributing water through pipes to radiators or underfloor heating systems. On the other hand, Steam Heating System uses steam to provide heat, which is delivered through pipes to radiators or convectors. Both systems offer efficient and environmentally friendly solutions for heating multiple buildings within a district.</p></p>
