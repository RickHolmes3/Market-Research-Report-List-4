<p><p>Thermopile Infrared (IR) sensors come in two main types: through-hole and surface-mount device (SMD). Through-hole thermopile infrared sensors are mounted by inserting the leads through pre-drilled holes on a PCB, making them more secure and stable. On the other hand, SMD thermopile infrared sensors are mounted on the surface of the PCB, offering a more compact and space-saving option. Both types of sensors are widely used in various applications such as temperature sensing, motion detection, and gas sensing.</p></p>
