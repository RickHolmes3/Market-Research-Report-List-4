<p><p>Waterproof fabrics are divided into two main types in the market: Breathable Waterproof Fabrics and Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics. Breathable waterproof fabrics are designed to allow air and moisture to pass through while repelling water, making them ideal for outdoor activities. On the other hand, non-breathable waterproof fabrics do not allow air or moisture to pass through, providing maximum protection against water but potentially causing discomfort due to lack of breathability. Both types serve specific purposes based on the level of waterproofing and breathability required.</p></p>
