<p><strong>The HVAC Temperature Sensors in Building Applicatinos Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>RTD</li><li>Thermocouples (TCs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC temperature sensors in building applications can be classified into various types, including NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient), PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient), RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector), Thermocouples (TCs), and others. NTC sensors have a higher resistance at lower temperatures, while PTC sensors have a higher resistance at higher temperatures. RTDs measure temperature based on the change in electrical resistance, while thermocouples rely on the voltage produced by the temperature difference between two dissimilar metals. Other types of sensors may include infrared sensors or semiconductor-based sensors.</p></p>
