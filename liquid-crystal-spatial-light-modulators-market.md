<p><strong>The Liquid Crystal Spatial Light Modulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators</li><li>Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators are devices that modulate light by reflecting incident light off a surface that changes its properties, such as phase or polarization. They are commonly used in applications such as holography and adaptive optics. Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators, on the other hand, modulate light by passing it through a transparent material that alters its phase or intensity. They are often used in imaging applications, such as displays and projectors. Both types of modulators play a crucial role in various optical technologies.</p></p>
