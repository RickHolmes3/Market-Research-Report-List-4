<p><strong>The Handheld VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoionization Detector (PID)</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor (MOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld VOC detectors are available in various types such as Photoionization Detector (PID), Flame Ionization Detector (FID), and Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor (MOS). PID detectors use ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, FID detectors use a flame to ionize VOCs, and MOS sensors detect VOCs based on changes in electrical conductivity. Each type of detector offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications, such as environmental monitoring, industrial hygiene, and safety assessments.</p></p>
