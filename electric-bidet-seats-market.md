<p><ul><li>Tank</li><li>Tankless</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric bidet seats come in three main types: tank, tankless, and hybrid. Tank bidet seats store warm water in a reservoir, providing a consistent temperature throughout the wash. Tankless bidet seats heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a reservoir and potentially offering unlimited warm water. Hybrid bidet seats combine features of both tank and tankless designs, offering the best of both worlds. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
