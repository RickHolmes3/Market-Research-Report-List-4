<p><p>Press fit machines are used to join components together by pressing them into place. There are three main types of press fit machines: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manual press fit machines require operators to manually load and press the components. Semi-automatic machines involve some level of automation in the pressing process, while fully-automatic machines are completely automated and require minimal human intervention. Each type of machine caters to different production needs and levels of automation in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
