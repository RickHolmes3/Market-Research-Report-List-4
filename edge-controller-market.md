<p><p>DIN-rail and Panel Mount edge controllers are two common market types in the industrial automation industry. DIN-rail controllers are designed to be mounted on standard DIN rails, making them ideal for space-saving installation in control cabinets. Panel mount controllers, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted directly on control panels or machinery. Both types of controllers offer reliable and robust control capabilities for a variety of industrial applications, but their form factors cater to different installation requirements.</p></p>
