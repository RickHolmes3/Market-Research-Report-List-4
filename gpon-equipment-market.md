<p><strong>The GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPON Equipment market consists of three main types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT), which acts as the concentrator and manages traffic sent from Optical Network Terminals (ONT); Optical Network Terminal (ONT), which is installed at the user's premise to convert optical signals into electrical signals for communication devices; and Passive Optical Splitters, which divide the optical signal to multiple users without needing power. These components work together to provide high-speed internet and other services through fiber optic networks.</p></p>
