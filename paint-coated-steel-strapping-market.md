<p><ul><li>Oscillated Wound Coil</li><li>Ribbon Would Coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint-coated steel strapping is available in two main market types: oscillated wound coil and ribbon wound coil. </p><p>Oscillated wound coil is wound in a tight, flat ribbon pattern, allowing for easy uncoiling and dispensing. This type is popular for use in automated packaging machines. </p><p>Ribbon wound coil is wound in a pancake-like shape, which reduces tangling and provides smooth feeding during manual application. This type is commonly used in industries where manual handling is required. Both types offer increased protection against corrosion and superior tensile strength for secure bundling and packaging applications.</p></p>
