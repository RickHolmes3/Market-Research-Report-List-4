<p><p>Air core reactors are used in electrical power systems for various applications. Two common types of air core reactors are the dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type air core reactors are insulated with materials like epoxy resin and are suitable for indoor applications. On the other hand, oil immersed type air core reactors are immersed in oil to provide better cooling and insulation, making them suitable for outdoor and high voltage applications. Both types are crucial components in power systems to control voltage and protect equipment.</p></p>
