<p><strong>The Mineral Insulated Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral Insulated Thermocouples are widely used in various industries and are categorized into three types based on the insulation configuration: Grounded Type, Ungrounded Type, and Exposed Type. </p><p>Grounded Type thermocouples have the thermocouple wires insulated from each other and the sheath to provide accurate temperature readings. Ungrounded Type thermocouples have the thermocouple wires insulated from each other but are in direct contact with the sheath. Exposed Type thermocouples have the thermocouple wires exposed at the tip for faster response times. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
