<p><ul><li>UV Mercury Lamp</li><li>UV LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra Violet Lamps are divided into two main types: UV Mercury Lamps and UV LED Lamps. UV Mercury Lamps produce UV light using mercury vapor, which requires a ballast for operation. They are widely used in various industrial applications such as water treatment and curing processes. On the other hand, UV LED Lamps use light-emitting diodes to produce UV light, offering a more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly alternative to mercury lamps. They are gaining popularity in applications such as sterilization, printing, and electronics.</p></p>
