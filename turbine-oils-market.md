<p><p>Turbine oils can be classified into two main types: Mineral Oil-Based Turbine Oils and Synthetic Oil-Based Turbine Oils. Mineral oil-based turbine oils are derived from refined petroleum products and offer good lubrication properties at a lower cost. On the other hand, synthetic oil-based turbine oils are chemically engineered oils that provide improved performance under extreme conditions, such as high temperatures and pressures. Both types of turbine oils cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in the market.</p></p>
