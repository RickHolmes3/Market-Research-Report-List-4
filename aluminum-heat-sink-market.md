<p><p>Aluminum heat sinks are essential components for managing heat in electronic devices. The market for aluminum heat sinks can be categorized into two types: passive heat sinks and active heat sinks. Passive heat sinks are simple, finned metal structures that rely on natural convection to dissipate heat. Active heat sinks, on the other hand, incorporate additional components like fans or heat pipes to enhance heat dissipation. Both types are crucial for maintaining the optimal operating temperature of electronic components.</p></p>
