<p><p>Throttle Position Sensors (TPS) are devices used in vehicles to monitor the position of the throttle valve. There are two main types of TPS in the market - End Switches Type and Potentiometer Type. End Switches Type TPS feature a mechanical switch that indicates when the throttle valve is fully open or closed. On the other hand, Potentiometer Type TPS use a variable resistor to track the throttle position, providing more precise data for engine control. Both types are essential for ensuring proper engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
