<p><p>Latex foam pads are broadly classified into two types - natural latex pads and synthetic latex pads. Natural latex pads are made from the sap of rubber trees and are known for their durability, breathability, and hypoallergenic properties. On the other hand, synthetic latex pads are man-made using a combination of petrochemicals and other additives. They are typically more affordable but may not offer the same level of durability and natural benefits as natural latex pads.</p></p>
