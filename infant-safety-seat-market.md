<p><ul><li>Rearward-facing baby seat</li><li>Combination seat (Rearward and Forward-facing)</li><li>Forward-facing child seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant safety seat market includes three main types: rearward-facing baby seats, combination seats (which can be used both rearward and forward-facing), and forward-facing child seats. Rearward-facing baby seats are designed to protect infants in the event of a collision by cradling them against the seat back. Combination seats offer the flexibility to be used as both rearward and forward-facing as the child grows. Forward-facing child seats are specifically designed for older children who have outgrown rearward-facing seats, providing protection in the event of a crash.</p></p>
