<p><p>Biodecontamination equipment includes technologies for eradicating biological contaminants in various environments. Chamber decontamination systems are designed for enclosed spaces like isolators, bio-safety cabinets, and incubators, while room decontamination systems are suitable for larger areas such as cleanrooms, laboratories, and hospital rooms. Chamber decontamination systems utilize hydrogen peroxide vapor or gaseous ozone, while room decontamination systems typically use aerosolized hydrogen peroxide or chlorine dioxide. Both types of equipment effectively eliminate bacteria, viruses, and fungi to ensure a safe and clean environment.</p></p>
