<p><p>A wave soldering machine is a type of soldering equipment used in the electronics assembly process to attach electronic components to a printed circuit board (PCB). The machine works by creating a wave of molten solder which flows over the PCB, soldering the components in place. Wave soldering machines are commonly used in mass production environments due to their efficiency and consistency in soldering large volumes of PCBs.</p><p>The Wave Soldering Machine Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
