<p><p>The road transport services market can be divided into two main types: Full Loads and Part Loads. Full Loads refer to shipments that occupy an entire truck, making it more cost-effective for the shipper as they pay for the entire truck space. Part Loads, on the other hand, refer to shipments that do not fill an entire truck and share space with other shipments. Part Loads are ideal for shippers with smaller quantities of goods, offering a more flexible and cost-efficient solution.</p></p>
