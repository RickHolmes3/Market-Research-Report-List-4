<p><p>Multi spot welding machines can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention from operators, such as loading parts and initiating the welding process, while fully automatic machines operate with minimal human involvement and can complete the entire welding process independently. Both types offer different levels of productivity and efficiency, with semi-automatic machines providing more control for delicate or complex welding tasks, while fully automatic machines are suitable for high-volume production environments.</p></p>
