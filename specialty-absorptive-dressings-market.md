<p><p>Specialty absorptive dressings are divided into two main types: non-adhering dressings and self-adhering dressings. Non-adhering dressings are designed to avoid sticking to the wound bed, reducing pain during dressing changes and minimizing trauma to the wound site. Self-adhering dressings, on the other hand, have an adhesive border that securely attaches to the skin, providing a barrier against bacteria and promoting the healing process. Both types of dressings are essential in wound care management, catering to different wound types and patient needs.</p></p>
