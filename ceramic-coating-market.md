Carbide coatings, like silicon carbide, are known for their hardness and abrasive resistance. Nitride coatings, such as titanium nitride, provide high wear resistance and low friction. Other types of coatings include boride and silicide, each with unique properties for specific applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
