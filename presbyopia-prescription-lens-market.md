<p><p>Presbyopia prescription lens market consists of three main types: single focus, bifocal, and other. Bifocal lenses have two separate areas for near and distance vision, making them ideal for individuals with presbyopia. The "other" market includes progressive lenses, trifocal lenses, and specialty lenses that offer additional features like glare reduction or blue light protection. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of individuals with presbyopia.</p></p>
