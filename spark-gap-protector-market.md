<p><p>Spark gap protectors are commonly available in three market types: Axial Leadwire Type, No Lead Type, and Radial Leadwire Type. Axial Leadwire Type protectors have leads extending from both ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting. No Lead Type protectors have no leads attached, ideal for surface mounting applications. Radial Leadwire Type protectors have leads extending from one side, making them suitable for soldering directly onto circuit boards. Each type caters to different installation requirements, providing flexibility for various applications.</p></p>
