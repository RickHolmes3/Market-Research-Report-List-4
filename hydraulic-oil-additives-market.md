<p><p>Hydraulic oil additives are categorized into three main types: antioxidants, anti-emulsifiers, and rust inhibitors. Antioxidants help in preventing oxidation of the oil, extending its lifespan. Anti-emulsifiers help in separating water from oil, ensuring better lubrication. Rust inhibitors protect metal surfaces from corrosion due to water or humidity, maintaining the efficiency of the hydraulic system. Each of these additives plays a crucial role in maintaining the performance and longevity of hydraulic equipment by addressing specific issues related to oxidation, emulsification, and rust formation.</p></p>
