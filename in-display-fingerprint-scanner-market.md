<p><p>The In Display Fingerprint Scanner market consists of three types: Optical, Ultrasonic, and Capacitive. </p><p>Optical scanners use light to map the unique fingerprint patterns, offering a quick and cost-effective solution. Ultrasonic scanners use sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, providing a more secure and accurate option. Capacitive scanners detect electrical current changes when a finger touches the sensor, offering a fast and reliable method of authentication. Each type caters to different levels of security and performance requirements in the market.</p></p>
