<p><p>Ozone monitors come in different types, with two common ones being UV photometric monitors and electrochemical monitors. UV photometric monitors measure ozone levels by detecting the amount of ultraviolet light absorbed by ozone molecules. On the other hand, electrochemical monitors use a chemical reaction to measure ozone levels. Both types of monitors are popular choices for monitoring ozone levels in various environments, such as industrial facilities, laboratories, and outdoor air quality monitoring stations.</p></p>
