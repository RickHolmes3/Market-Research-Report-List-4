<p><p>Felt-tip pens can be categorized into three main types based on the type of ink they use - oily, water-based, and alcoholic felt-tip pens. Oily felt-tip pens have ink that consists of oils and dyes, providing a smooth writing experience. Water-based felt-tip pens use water-soluble ink that is easily washable. Alcoholic felt-tip pens contain ink with alcohol as a solvent, which dries quickly and is ideal for use on non-porous surfaces. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
