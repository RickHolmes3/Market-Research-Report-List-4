<p><ul><li>Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL)</li><li>Ultrasonic Test (UT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent pigging services utilize tools like Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) and Ultrasonic Testing (UT) to inspect pipelines for defects and anomalies without disassembling them. MFL technology uses magnetic fields to detect metal loss, while UT technology sends high-frequency sound waves through the pipeline to analyze wall thickness. Both methods are non-destructive and provide accurate results on the condition of the pipeline. Different industries utilize these services to ensure the integrity and safety of their pipelines.</p></p>
