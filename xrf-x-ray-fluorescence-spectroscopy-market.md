<p><p>XRF (X-ray Fluorescence Spectroscopy) market includes handheld and desktop analyzers. Handheld XRF analyzers are portable and used for on-site analysis, making them popular in industries like mining, metal recycling, and environmental monitoring. Desktop XRF analyzers are larger and offer higher precision for lab-based analysis, making them suitable for research institutions and quality control labs. Both types of analyzers use X-ray fluorescence to determine the elemental composition of a sample by measuring the fluorescent X-rays emitted when the sample is irradiated.</p></p>
