<p><strong>The Mercury Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer are two types of mercury analyzers that are used to detect and measure mercury levels in various samples. The Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer works by converting elemental mercury into vapor, which is then quantified using atomic absorption spectroscopy. On the other hand, the Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer operates by converting elemental mercury into fluorescent mercury vapor, which is measured using atomic fluorescence spectroscopy. Both analyzers are commonly used in environmental, industrial, and healthcare settings for accurate and sensitive mercury detection.</p></p>
