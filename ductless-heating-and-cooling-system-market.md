<p><p>Ductless heating and cooling systems, also known as mini-split systems, are highly efficient HVAC systems that provide both heating and cooling in a single unit. These systems consist of an outdoor compressor unit and one or more indoor air handlers, allowing for customizable zone heating and cooling in different areas of a home or building. Ductless systems are popular for their energy efficiency, ease of installation, and flexibility in controlling the temperature of individual rooms.</p><p>The ductless heating and cooling system market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for energy-efficient HVAC solutions, rising awareness about the benefits of ductless systems, and the growing trend towards smart and connected homes. Additionally, the shift towards sustainable and eco-friendly heating and cooling solutions is driving the adoption of ductless systems in residential and commercial buildings.</p><p>Technological advancements such as inverter-driven compressors, improved energy efficiency ratings, and enhanced control features are some of the latest trends in the ductless heating and cooling system market. Manufacturers are also focusing on developing multi-zone systems that can provide heating and cooling in multiple rooms or areas, further driving the growth of the market.</p></p>
