<p><p>Respiratory Assist Devices (RADs) are medical devices used to help individuals with respiratory disorders breathe more easily. These devices provide respiratory support by assisting with the inhalation and exhalation process, often through the use of continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP) or bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) therapy. RADs are commonly used to treat conditions such as sleep apnea, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), and respiratory failure.</p><p>The Respiratory Assist Devices Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.20% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of respiratory disorders, technological advancements in RADs, and growing awareness about the importance of respiratory health. Additionally, the rising geriatric population and the increasing demand for home-based respiratory care are driving the market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Respiratory Assist Devices Market include the development of portable and wearable RADs, advancements in telemedicine for remote monitoring of patients using RADs, and the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to enhance the functionality of these devices. Overall, the market for respiratory assist devices is expected to continue expanding in the coming years as the need for effective respiratory support solutions grows.</p></p>
