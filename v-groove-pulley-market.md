<p><p>V-groove pulleys come in three main types: double groove, single groove, and multiple groove. Double groove pulleys have two V-shaped grooves for use with two belts, providing increased torque capacity. Single groove pulleys have one V-shaped groove for one belt, suitable for applications with lower power transmission requirements. Multiple groove pulleys have more than two V-shaped grooves and are used with multiple belts for higher power transmission needs. Each type caters to different market demands based on the required torque capacity and power transmission capabilities.</p></p>
