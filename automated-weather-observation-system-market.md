<p><p>Automated Weather Observation System (AWOS) market includes two major types- Surface Weather and High Altitude Weather systems. Surface Weather systems primarily focus on providing real-time weather data at ground level, such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and visibility. On the other hand, High Altitude Weather systems are designed to monitor and forecast weather conditions at higher altitudes, particularly for aviation purposes. Both types of AWOS systems play a crucial role in enhancing safety and efficiency in various industries such as aviation, transportation, and agriculture.</p></p>
