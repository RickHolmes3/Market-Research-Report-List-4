<p><p>Small business network switches come in two main market types: fixed port switches and modular switches. Fixed port switches have a set number of ports that cannot be changed or expanded, making them ideal for small businesses with straightforward networking needs. Modular switches, on the other hand, offer more flexibility by allowing users to add or remove modules to customize the number of ports and features based on their specific requirements. Both types cater to the diverse networking needs of small businesses.</p></p>
