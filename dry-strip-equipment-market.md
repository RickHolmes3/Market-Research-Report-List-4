<p><p>Dry strip equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to remove photoresist and other materials from wafers without using liquid chemicals. There are two main types of dry strip equipment - those with 1 process chamber and those with 2 process chambers. </p><p>1 Process Chamber equipment uses a single chamber for the entire stripping process, while 2 Process Chamber equipment uses separate chambers for plasma generation and substrate processing. The latter is often preferred for higher throughput and better process control, while the former is more cost-effective for smaller-scale operations.</p></p>
