<p><p>Masking Protective Film is broadly categorized into two types: Solid Masking Protective Film and Liquid Masking Protective Film. Solid Masking Protective Film is a peelable protective coating that is applied in a solid form and can easily be removed after use. On the other hand, Liquid Masking Protective Film is a liquid coating that dries to form a protective barrier before being peeled off. Both types of protective films are used for protecting surfaces during various industrial processes, painting, and construction activities.</p></p>
