<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Triple Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transcutaneous pacing market includes three types of temporary pacemakers. Single chamber temporary pacemakers stimulate one chamber of the heart, typically the ventricle. Dual chamber temporary pacemakers stimulate both the atrium and the ventricle of the heart. Triple chamber temporary pacemakers are capable of stimulating the atria, one ventricle, and the other ventricle independently. Each type of temporary pacemaker is used in different clinical situations depending on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
