<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Technology Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic wave technology sensors are categorized into two main types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors utilize waves that travel along the surface of a material, while BAW sensors use waves that penetrate through the material in bulk. SAW sensors are known for their high sensitivity and low cost, making them ideal for various applications such as gas sensing and pressure monitoring. On the other hand, BAW sensors offer high frequency capabilities and are commonly used in telecommunications and RF filters.</p></p>
