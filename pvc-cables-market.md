<p><p>PVC cables are widely used in two main market types: medium voltage cables and low voltage cables. Medium voltage cables are designed to carry electrical power at medium voltage levels, typically ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV. They are used in various applications such as power distribution networks and industrial installations. On the other hand, low voltage cables are used for distributing power at lower voltage levels, typically up to 1 kV. They are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings for various electrical installations.</p></p>
