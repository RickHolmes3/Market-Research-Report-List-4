<p><p>Pressure balanced expansion joints are used in pipelines to absorb thermal expansion and reduce stress on the system. The two main types of pressure balanced expansion joints are inline and elbow. Inline expansion joints are used in straight sections of piping, while elbow type expansion joints are used in areas where there is a change in direction, such as at a bend or elbow. Both types of expansion joints help to maintain the integrity of the pipeline system and ensure maximum efficiency and safety.</p></p>
