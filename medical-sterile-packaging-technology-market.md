<p><ul><li>Chemical Sterilization</li><li>Physical Sterilization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical sterile packaging technology market involves two main types of sterilization methods: chemical sterilization and physical sterilization. Chemical sterilization utilizes chemicals such as ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide to kill microorganisms on the packaging materials. On the other hand, physical sterilization involves methods like irradiation or steam sterilization to achieve the same goal. Both types of sterilization are essential in ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical devices and pharmaceutical products that are packaged for distribution and use.</p></p>
