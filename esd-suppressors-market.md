<p><strong>The ESD Suppressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diode Array</li><li>Polymer</li><li>TVS</li><li>Zener</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ESD suppressors are used to protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are four main types of ESD suppressors: diode array, polymer, TVS, and Zener. Diode array suppressors utilize multiple diodes to provide protection. Polymer suppressors are made from polymer materials and offer effective ESD protection. TVS suppressors use a transient voltage suppressor to divert excess energy away from the device. Zener suppressors use Zener diodes to regulate voltage and protect against ESD. Each type offers unique advantages in protecting electronic devices.</p></p>
