<p><p>Rack-mount and wall-mount are two main types of lighting control dimming panels in the market. Rack-mount panels are installed in a rack system and are typically used in larger commercial or industrial settings. They provide centralized control and monitoring of lighting systems. On the other hand, wall-mount panels are directly mounted on a wall surface and are commonly used in smaller residential or commercial spaces. They offer convenient access to dimming controls and are more suitable for less complex lighting setups.</p></p>
