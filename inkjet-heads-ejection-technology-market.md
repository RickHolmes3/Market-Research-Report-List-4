<p><strong>The Inkjet Heads Ejection Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Heads</li><li>Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Inkjet Heads Ejection Technology market, there are two main types: Continuous Inkjet Heads and Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads. Continuous Inkjet Heads continuously eject tiny droplets of ink through a nozzle, allowing for high-speed and high-volume printing. Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads, on the other hand, only eject ink droplets when needed, resulting in more precise and controlled printing. Both technologies have their own advantages and are used in various industries for printing applications.</p></p>
