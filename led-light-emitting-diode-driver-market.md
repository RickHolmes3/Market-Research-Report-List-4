<p><strong>The LED Light emitting Diode Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Voltage</li><li>Constant Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Light emitting Diode Drivers are categorized into two main types: Constant Voltage and Constant Current drivers. Constant Voltage drivers regulate the voltage supplied to the LED, ensuring a consistent voltage output regardless of fluctuations in input voltage. Constant Current drivers, on the other hand, maintain a steady current flow to the LED, preventing variations in current that could potentially damage the LED. Each type of driver is suited for specific applications based on the requirements of the LED lighting system.</p></p>
