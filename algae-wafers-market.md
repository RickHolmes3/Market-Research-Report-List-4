<p><ul><li>Sinking Particles</li><li>Non-sinking Particles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Algae wafers come in two main market types: sinking particles and non-sinking particles. Sinking particles are designed to sink to the bottom of the tank, making them ideal for bottom-dwelling fish like catfish and loaches. On the other hand, non-sinking particles remain buoyant in the water, making them suitable for fish that feed at the surface or in the middle of the tank. Both types of algae wafers provide essential nutrients for herbivorous fish and help promote a balanced diet in an aquarium setting.</p></p>
