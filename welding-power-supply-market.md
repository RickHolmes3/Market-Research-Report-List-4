<p><ul><li>Direct Current</li><li>Alternating Current</li><li>Pulsed Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are three main types of welding power supplies: direct current (DC), alternating current (AC), and pulsed current. DC welding power supplies provide a constant flow of electricity in one direction, making them suitable for welding materials like steel. AC welding power supplies alternate the direction of the current, making them ideal for welding aluminum and magnesium. Pulsed current welding power supplies alternate between high and low current levels, allowing for better control over the welding process and producing high-quality welds on thin materials.</p></p>
