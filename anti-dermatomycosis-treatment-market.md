<p><p>Anti-dermatomycosis treatment market types include polyenes, azoles, allylamines, echinocandins, and others. Polyenes work by binding to the fungal cell membrane, causing pores to form and leading to cell death. Azoles inhibit the synthesis of ergosterol, a vital component of fungal cell membranes. Allylamines disrupt the synthesis of ergosterol as well. Echinocandins interfere with cell wall synthesis. Other types of treatment include combination therapies and newer agents targeting specific pathways in the fungal cell.</p></p>
