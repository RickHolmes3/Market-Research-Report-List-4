<p><p>Natural surgical glue can be classified into three main types: Fibrin glue, Collagen glue, and Gelatin glue. </p><p>Fibrin glue is made from fibrinogen and thrombin, which are proteins found in blood plasma, and is used to promote blood clotting and tissue healing. Collagen glue is derived from animal collagen and is used for wound closure and tissue repair. Gelatin glue is made from the protein gelatin and is commonly used in surgical procedures to seal tissues and prevent leakage. Each type of natural surgical glue has unique properties and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
