<p><ul><li>Single-Shaft Shredders</li><li>Dual-Shaft Shredders</li><li>Four-Shaft Shredders</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal shredders can be categorized into several types based on the number of shafts they have. Single-shaft shredders have a single rotating shaft that cuts and shreds materials. Dual-shaft shredders have two counter-rotating shafts for increased efficiency and throughput. Four-shaft shredders have four shafts that work together to thoroughly shred materials into smaller pieces. Other types of shredders may include specialty shredders designed for specific materials or applications, such as industrial shredders, high-torque shredders, or plastic shredders.</p></p>
