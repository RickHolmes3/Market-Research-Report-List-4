<p><ul><li>GEO</li><li>MEO</li><li>LEO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home Satellite Internet Services can be classified into three main market types based on the altitude of the satellites: GEO (Geostationary Earth Orbit), MEO (Medium Earth Orbit), and LEO (Low Earth Orbit). GEO satellites are positioned farther from Earth, providing wide coverage but slower speeds. MEO satellites are positioned closer, offering faster speeds but limited coverage. LEO satellites orbit closest to Earth, providing low latency and high speeds but requiring a larger number of satellites for global coverage. Each market type has its own advantages and limitations for delivering internet services to homes.</p></p>
