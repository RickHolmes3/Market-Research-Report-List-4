<p><p>Thermoelectric Modules and Assemblies market can be categorized into two main types: Single Stage Modules and Multistage Modules. Single Stage Modules utilize only one thermoelectric material to convert heat to electricity, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Multistage Modules consist of multiple thermoelectric materials stacked together, allowing for higher efficiency and better performance, particularly in applications requiring precise temperature control. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market, offering varying levels of performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
