<p><p>Laboratory Masticator Market offers two main types: Paddle Masticator and Ultrasonic Masticator. Paddle Masticators use rotating paddles to mix and homogenize samples, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Ultrasonic Masticators use high-frequency sound waves to break down samples, making them ideal for delicate or heat-sensitive samples. Both types of masticators are widely used in research labs and industrial settings for preparing samples for analysis and testing.</p></p>
