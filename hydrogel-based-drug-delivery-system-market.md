<p><strong>The Hydrogel-based Drug Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural System</li><li>Synthesis System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogel-based drug delivery systems are categorized into two main types: natural and synthesis systems. </p><p>- Natural systems use naturally occurring hydrogels such as alginate or chitosan, which are derived from sources like seaweed or shrimp shells. These systems are biocompatible and biodegradable, making them suitable for various applications in drug delivery.</p><p>- Synthesis systems involve the creation of hydrogels through chemical processes, allowing for more precise control over properties such as swelling, degradation, and drug release kinetics. These systems can be tailored to specific requirements for effective drug delivery.</p></p>
