<p><p>Liquid macrofiltration market types include granular micro filter, filter press filter, leaf tubular and press filter, bag filter, drum and disk filter, belt filter press, and AWB filter. Granular micro filters utilize small granules to trap particles in liquid. Filter press filters use pressure to separate solids from liquids. Leaf tubular and press filters use mesh screens to filter liquids. Bag filters use fabric bags to capture particles. Drum and disk filters rotate to filter liquids. Belt filter presses use belts to separate solids from liquids. AWB filters use a combination of air, water, and biological processes to filter liquids.</p></p>
