<p><p>Hybrid Polymer Capacitors are available in two main types: Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Radial Lead Type. SMD capacitors are designed for surface mounting on printed circuit boards, offering space-saving and efficient performance. On the other hand, Radial Lead Type capacitors have leads that extend from either end, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in electronic devices. Both types are widely used in various applications due to their high capacitance values, low ESR, and long service life.</p></p>
