<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Transformers</li><li>Pad-mounted Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small distribution transformers are available in two main market types, pole-mounted transformers and pad-mounted transformers. Pole-mounted transformers are typically installed on utility poles and are commonly used in residential neighborhoods. They are easy to access for maintenance and repairs but can be affected by storms and other external factors. Pad-mounted transformers are installed on the ground in a protective casing, making them more secure and durable. They are often used in commercial and industrial settings where space is limited and aesthetics are a concern.</p></p>
