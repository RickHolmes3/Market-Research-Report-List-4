<p><p>Axle counter systems are used in railway signaling to detect trains and track their movements. There are two main types of installations for these systems: rail side installation and on-rail installation. In rail side installation, the axle counters are installed beside the track, while in on-rail installation, the counters are mounted directly onto the rail. Both types of installations serve the same purpose of accurately counting the number of axles passing a certain point on the railway track, ensuring safe and efficient train operations.</p></p>
