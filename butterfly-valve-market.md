<p><p>A butterfly valve is a type of quarter-turn valve that controls the flow of a fluid with a circular disc or vane that rotates on an axis perpendicular to the flow. The design of the valve allows for quick and efficient shut-off as well as flow control. Butterfly valves are commonly used in applications where reliability and quick shut-off are important, such as in industrial applications, water supply systems, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The global butterfly valve market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to factors such as increasing demand for butterfly valves in various industries, growing investments in water and wastewater treatment infrastructure, and advancements in valve technology. Additionally, the rise in urbanization and industrialization in emerging economies is expected to drive the demand for butterfly valves.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the butterfly valve market include the adoption of smart valves with built-in sensors for monitoring and control, the development of corrosion-resistant materials for valves used in harsh environments, and the integration of automation and control systems for efficient valve operation. Overall, the butterfly valve market is expected to continue to grow as industries increasingly focus on improving efficiency and reliability of fluid control systems.</p></p>
