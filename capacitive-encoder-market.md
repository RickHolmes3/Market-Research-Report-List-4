<p><p>Capacitive encoders are available in two main types- linear encoders and rotary encoders. Linear encoders are used to measure linear movement, typically in applications like machine tools, robotics, and semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, rotary encoders are used to measure angular rotation, commonly found in motor control systems, robotics, and industrial automation. Both types of encoders use capacitance sensing technology to provide precise and accurate position feedback, making them essential components in various motion control systems.</p></p>
