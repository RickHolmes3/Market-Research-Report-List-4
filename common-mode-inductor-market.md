<p><ul><li>SMD Common Mode Inductor</li><li>Through Hole Common Mode Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMD Common Mode Inductor market refers to surface mount device inductors that are small in size and can be easily soldered onto a printed circuit board. Through Hole Common Mode Inductor market includes inductors that are inserted through holes on a PCB for added stability and durability. Both types of inductors are used to suppress electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits by filtering out common mode noise signals. They are commonly used in a wide range of electronic devices such as computers, televisions, and power supplies.</p></p>
