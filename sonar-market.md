<p><p>Sonar technology is used in various market types to gather underwater data efficiently. Single Beam Scanning Sonar Systems provide a narrow, high-resolution image of the seafloor. Multi-beam Sonar Systems offer a wider coverage area, ideal for mapping larger underwater areas. Side Scan Sonar Systems produce detailed images of the seafloor and any objects in view. Other markets for sonar technology include Forward-looking Sonar Systems for navigation and Obstacle Avoidance Sonar Systems for underwater vehicles. Each market type serves a specific purpose in underwater exploration and navigation.</p></p>
