<p><strong>The Power Distribution Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HV</li><li>MV</li><li>LV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power distribution cables market can be categorized into three main types - High Voltage (HV), Medium Voltage (MV), and Low Voltage (LV). HV cables are used for long-distance transmission of electricity at high voltages, typically ranging from 66 kV to 500 kV. MV cables are used for medium-range power distribution within industrial complexes and residential areas, commonly operating at voltages of 6 kV to 33 kV. LV cables are designed for low-voltage distribution networks, serving buildings and small-scale installations with voltages below 1 kV.</p></p>

<p><p>Power distribution cables are used in three main applications: Overland, Underground, and Submarine. Underground cables are buried below the surface and are ideal for urban areas or environmentally sensitive areas. Submarine cables are laid underwater to connect islands or countries separated by water. Each application requires different cable characteristics to ensure efficient power distribution and transmission.</p></p>
