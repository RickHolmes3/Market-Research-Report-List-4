<p><p>The Telecom Power System market can be classified into two types: On Grid and Off Grid. On Grid systems are connected to the main power grid and rely on utility power to operate. They are suitable for areas with reliable grid infrastructure. On the other hand, Off Grid systems are independent and do not rely on the main power grid. They are suitable for remote locations where grid connection is not feasible, utilizing alternative power sources such as solar panels or diesel generators to meet energy needs.</p></p>
