Semi-continuous systems involve intermittent processing, where batches of tea leaves are loaded and extracted in separate cycles. On the other hand, continuous systems enable a steady and uninterrupted extraction process, allowing for higher efficiency and output. Both systems cater to different production scales and requirements, providing options for tea manufacturers to choose the most suitable equipment based on their specific needs and capacities.</p></p>
