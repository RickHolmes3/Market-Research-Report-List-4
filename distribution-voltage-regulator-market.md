<p><strong>The Distribution Voltage Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant Switching</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distribution Voltage Regulator Market consists of two main types: Ferroresonant and Tap Switching. Ferroresonant regulators use a ferroresonant transformer to stabilize voltage by adjusting the input current. They are known for their reliability and efficiency. Tap Switching regulators, on the other hand, adjust the transformer taps to vary the output voltage. They are preferred for their flexibility in responding to changing load conditions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring a stable voltage supply in distribution networks.</p></p>
