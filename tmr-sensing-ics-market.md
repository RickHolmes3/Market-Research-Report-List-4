<p><ul><li>Analog TMR Sensing ICs</li><li>Digital TMR Sensing ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog TMR (tunneling magneto-resistance) sensing ICs are used for applications where precise and continuous measurement of magnetism is required. They provide a smooth output signal proportional to the strength of the magnetic field. On the other hand, digital TMR sensing ICs convert the analog signal into a digital output, making them suitable for applications where discrete measurements are needed. Both types of TMR sensing ICs are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation for magnetic field detection and sensing applications.</p></p>
