<p><p>Vented drip chambers are used in medical IV tubing to control the flow of medication or fluids into the patient. The two main types of vented drip chambers are Macro Drip and Micro Drip. Macro Drip chambers are designed for delivering larger volumes of fluids at a faster rate, typically used in emergency situations. Micro Drip chambers are used for delivering smaller volumes of fluids at a slower rate, ideal for precise medication dosing or continuous infusion therapy. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and safe delivery of fluids to patients.</p></p>
