<p><p>Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) are commonly categorized into two types: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing. Fixed Wing UAS have a design similar to traditional airplanes, featuring wings that provide lift and propulsion. These UAS are known for their long endurance and large coverage area, making them ideal for surveillance and mapping missions. On the other hand, Rotary Wing UAS, also known as drones, have vertical take-off and landing capabilities, enabling them to hover and maneuver in tighter spaces, making them suitable for tasks like inspection and search and rescue operations.</p></p>
