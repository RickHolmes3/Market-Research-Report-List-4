<p><p>A thermoelectric generator (TEG) module is a device that converts heat energy into electrical energy. TEG modules can be classified into two main types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single stage modules consist of a single pair of p-type and n-type thermoelectric materials, while multistage modules have multiple pairs arranged in series to increase the efficiency of heat to electricity conversion. Both types of TEG modules serve as sustainable and reliable sources of power generation in various applications.</p></p>
