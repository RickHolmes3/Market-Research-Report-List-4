<p><p>Vibratory conveyors market can be classified into different types based on the different types of springs used in the conveyor system. Coil spring conveyors use coil springs for vibration isolation. Rubber spring conveyors use rubber springs for cushioning the vibrations. S slat conveyors use slats to transport materials. Shear mount conveyors use shear mounts to reduce vibration transmission. Other types of vibratory conveyors may include air-spring conveyors, leaf spring conveyors, and tuned mass damper conveyors, each offering unique benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
