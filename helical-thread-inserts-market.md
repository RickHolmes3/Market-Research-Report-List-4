<p><p>Helical thread inserts are used to strengthen and repair threads in materials like metal or plastic. There are two main types of helical thread inserts: Tanged thread inserts and Tangless thread inserts. Tanged inserts have a tang at the end which is broken off after installation, providing added security. Tangless inserts do not have a tang, making installation easier and eliminating the risk of tang breakage. Both types offer different advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
