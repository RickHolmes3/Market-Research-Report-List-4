<p><p>Industrial centrifugal blowers are commonly classified based on their suspension system, including air suspension blowers and magnetic suspension blowers. Air suspension blowers use air bearings to suspend the rotor, which reduces friction and enables high speeds and efficiency. On the other hand, magnetic suspension blowers use magnetic bearings, which provide stable suspension without contact between parts, resulting in lower maintenance and longer lifespan. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
