Two common types are Shell & Tube Type and Immersion Type. Shell & Tube Type UHP Heat Exchangers consist of a series of tubes housed within a larger shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer. Immersion Type UHP Heat Exchangers immerse the heating or cooling element directly into the process fluid. Both types are crucial in maintaining precise temperature control in UHP applications.</p></p>
