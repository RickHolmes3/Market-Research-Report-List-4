<p><strong>The Bacterial Endotoxin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bacterial endotoxin testing market offers three main types of tests: Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test. Gel Clot test detects endotoxins by formation of gel in the presence of endotoxin. Chromogenic test utilizes a color change reaction to detect endotoxins. Turbidimetric test measures changes in turbidity caused by the reaction of endotoxins. These tests are crucial in ensuring safety of pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and cosmetics by detecting harmful bacterial endotoxins.</p></p>
